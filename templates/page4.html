How are Job Losses Distributed Between Industries?

This page will show how job lossses distribute across different industries, this part of the slideshow is meant for you to explore the data on your own. So go ahead and try selecting your state to see how it fared in regard to job losses. Then take so time to explore other states to see how they compare.

ℹ Select a state from the drop down menu to see how the Job losses distrubute across industries for that state. Hover over any of the Bars to see the actual number of jobs lost.
ℹ Remember a negative number of jobs lost means that that industry actually gained jobs.
ℹPlease note that the bar lengths are not always proportional to the actual number of jobs lost due to the wide range of values for the number of jobs lost, some bars may be the same length but but will represent different numbers of jobs lost
